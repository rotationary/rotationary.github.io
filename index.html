Rotationary

On-Demand
Aerial Photos and Videos

Rotationary was founded to provide drone services to the consumer market. We seamlessly match available drones with consumers who need aerial photos and video. Our on demand service provides you with the advantages of drones without the substantial investment in equipment and training.

Ordering a drone is as simple as logging into our app and scheduling the desired time. Rotationary's database of drone pilots will match you up according to your specific needs. Our online tools allow you to review the files and post them on social media. Rotationary's advanced interface pinpoints where you want the drone to go and sends it your way.
